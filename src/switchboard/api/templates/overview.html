Why it’s safe to leave on six gates before production

Human approval

Nothing reaches production without a person signing off — and the sign-off is recorded.

Dry-run by default

Actions simulate first. A live write needs an explicitly approved item.

Spend governor

Hard caps on API, model & warehouse spend — per run and per day.

Verified facts only

Research search-confirms a claim, or it stays labeled a claim.

Read-only paid media

The ad platforms are never written to — no bid, budget or campaign changes.

Draft + human-send

Digests and posts are assembled for review, never published automatically.
